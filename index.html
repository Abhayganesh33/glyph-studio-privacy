Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026 · Glyph Studio · Android
01

Introduction

Glyph Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is an Android application designed exclusively for Nothing Phone devices to control the Glyph LED interface.

02

Information We Collect

Glyph Studio does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data stays on your device.

03

Permissions We Use

RECORD_AUDIO
Microphone

Used exclusively for the Music Visualizer feature to detect ambient sound and sync Glyph LEDs. Audio is processed locally on your device and never recorded or transmitted.

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION
Location

Used exclusively for the Glyph Compass feature to determine the device's heading direction and sync Glyph LEDs accordingly. Location data is processed locally on your device and never stored or transmitted.

BODY_SENSORS
Body Sensors

Used to access the device's magnetometer sensor for the Glyph Compass feature. Sensor data is processed locally and never stored or transmitted.

INTERNET
Network

Used to fetch dynamic application typography via Google Fonts and handle external link navigation for support pages. No personal user data or tracking metrics are transmitted over the network.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Background

Used to keep Glyph LED features (torch, battery indicator, timer) active in the background. A persistent notification is shown while the service is running.

WAKE_LOCK
System

Prevents the device from sleeping while Glyph LED animations are active.

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
System

Allows the background service to restart automatically after the device reboots.

04

Data Storage

All user preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. No data is sent to external servers.

05

Third Party Services

Glyph Studio does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

06

Nothing Phone SDK

Glyph Studio uses the Nothing Glyph SDK to control LED lights on Nothing Phone devices. This SDK does not collect or transmit any user data.

07

Children's Privacy

Glyph Studio does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
